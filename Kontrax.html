About Kontrax

Objective

Kontrax was built from an awareness of how complex and frustrating contract management can become.

We wanted an affordable cloud-based solution that would help businesses like yours.

Not being able to find the right contract or easily access terms and conditions can lead to contract penalties, frustrated clients, lost revenue, and missed opportunities.

01

Kontrax Helps In...

Goal

Kontrax is a contract management tool that helps you to streamline and simplify the contracting process. With Kontrax, you can easily coordinate all contract-related activities within your team, ensure important documents are signed, and get secure access to your contracts from anywhere in the world. This software can improve your team's efficiency and effectiveness by providing a centralized platform to manage your contract database, track progress, and collaborate with stakeholders..

The Solution

Features

Kontrax also offers the ability to add contracts and suppliers to its platform. This allows you to keep all your contracts and suppliers organized in one place, making it easier to manage and track important information.

With Kontrax, you can download, email, and print your contracts with just a few clicks. This makes it easy to share contracts with stakeholders and have a physical backup of important documents.

As an online platform, Kontrax is easily accessed and shared with stakeholders. You can give collaborators secure access to specific contracts or suppliers, making it easy to work together remotely.

In addition, Kontrax provides contract insights that give you business knowledge at your fingertips. You can quickly see key details about your contracts, such as their value, renewal dates, and any outstanding obligations. This helps you to stay on top of your contracts and make informed decisions.
